About Us

Guangzhou Covano Logistics Technology Co., Ltd. is a fully licensed logistics company approved by China's Ministry of Commerce and the Administration for Industry and Commerce. We specialize in international air freight, ocean shipping, and international express delivery, while also providing comprehensive logistics solutions including warehousing, customs clearance, and related services. After years of dedicated development, we have built a professional team with extensive experience and expertise in logistics operations, maintaining strong industry standards and a solid reputation for reliability. Additionally, we have established a well-developed global freight service network. Guided by our operational philosophy of "Enhancing Service, Delivering Value", our entire team is committed to providing customers worldwide with professional, secure, and efficient international logistics solutions through our international network, along with value-added services.


Leveraging the dense international flight networks of Hong Kong Airport, Shenzhen Airport, and Guangzhou Airport, along with high-efficiency transit capabilities, we provide unparalleled air cargo solutions. As a top-tier regional agent in South China for multiple major airlines—including CZ (China Southern), HU (Hainan Airlines), MH (Malaysia Airlines), EK (Emirates), TK (Turkish Airlines), EY (Etihad), ET (Ethiopian Airlines), and SQ (Singapore Airlines)—we handle over 100 tons of cargo monthly. Our real-time airline system integration enables instant tracking of shipments, allowing us to respond swiftly and accurately to dynamic logistics needs. This proactive approach ensures optimal efficiency and competitive advantages for our clients


The global network of international freight forwarding agents extends Covano Logistics' services to 188 countries and regions worldwide. Covano International Logistics provides comprehensive services, including transportation to major airport cities worldwide, transit, and import transportation, offering the most robust support to meet the diverse needs of different clients.


Years of practice have proven that BinHong Logistics' development strategy of "professional, standardized, and international logistics," along with its steady operational approach, aligns with the trends of global logistics, laying a solid foundation for the company's future growth. The company will further strengthen its cooperation with airlines, remain market-oriented, enhance management, and drive innovation to continuously improve service quality. Adhering to the principle of "professional service, earning customer trust," the corporate philosophy of "mastery across the seas, sincerity reaching the world," and the code of conduct "cherishing every single client commitment," we are dedicated to providing our customers with convenient, efficient, and premium logistics services.
