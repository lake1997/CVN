Corporate Profile

Guangzhou Covano Logistics Technology Co., Ltd. is an international logistics provider approved by the Ministry of Commerce and registered with the State Administration for Market Regulation. Specializing in air freight, ocean shipping, and international express services, we offer comprehensive logistics solutions encompassing warehousing, customs clearance, and value-added services. With years of industry experience, our team of seasoned professionals delivers tailored logistics strategies to meet diverse client needs. We uphold rigorous industry standards and ethical practices, supported by a global network of partners ensuring seamless cross-border operations. Guided by our mission to "Enhance Service, Maximize Value", we leverage our international presence to provide professional, secure, and efficient logistics solutions, empowering businesses worldwide with reliable supply chain management.
